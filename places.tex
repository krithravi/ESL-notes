Unas frases con lugares y posiciones relativas:
\begin{itemize}
	\item The pharmacy is on the left of the bakery.
		\arr \ita{La farmacia est\'a a la izquierda de la panader\'ia.}
	\item The restaurant is between the pharmacy and the supermarket.
		\arr \ita{El restaurante est\'a entre la farmacia y el supermercado.}
	\item The cinema is diagonal to the gas station.
		\arr \ita{El cine est\'a diagonal a la gasolinera.}
	\item The bakery is in front of the hospital.
		\arr \ita{La panader\'a est\'a enfrente del hospital.}
	\item The bookshop is near the library.
		\arr \ita{La librer\'ia est\'a cerca de la biblioteca.}
	\item The school is behind the bank.
		\arr \ita{La escuela est\'a detr\'as del banco.}
	\item The people are outside the school.
		\arr \ita{La gente est\'a fuera de la escuela.}
	\item There is a pharmacy near the supermarket.
		\arr \ita{Hay una farmacia cerca del supermercado.}
\end{itemize}
