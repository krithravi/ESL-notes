Let's take a look at a few examples:

\begin{itemize}
	\item My mother's mother is my grandmother.
		\arr \ita{La madre de mi madre es mi abuela.}
	\item My father's brother is my uncle.
		\arr \ita{El hermano de mi padre es mi t\'io.}
	\item My son's sister is my daughter.
		\arr \ita{La hermana de mi hijo es mi hija.}
	\item My mother's father is my grandfather.
		\arr \ita{El padre de mi madre es mi abuelo.}
	\item My mother's sister is my aunt.
		\arr \ita{La hermana de mi madre es mi t\'ia.}
\end{itemize}
